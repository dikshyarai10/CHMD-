Teacher Training

Shape the Future with Montessori Education!

Children’s House Montessori Elementary School, Dharan, offers CTEVT-affiliated
teacher training courses to empower aspiring educators with the skills and expertise
needed to excel in Montessori education.

Why choose our Training Programs?

For over 10 years, Children’s House Montessori Elementary School, Dharan, has been a trusted provider of Montessori teacher training. Our programs combine
proven methodologies, practical classroom techniques, and a child-centered approach to education. With a decade of excellence, we have empowered
countless educators to transform learning environments and make a lasting impact on young minds.

Junior Diploma

3 months Junior Diploma with free 1 day workshop
(Teachers Role, Personality development, Arts & Craft, etc)

We provide training, set-up by Maria Montessori herself.
You’ll learn child development and psychology and how
to deliver the unique Montessori approach.

Senior Diploma

The advanced Diploma in Montessori Method of Education
prepares educators to teach children aged 2–6 using the
Montessori curriculum and methodology. This rigorous course
imparts in-depth knowledge of Montessori theory, philosophy,
and practices for early childhood education.
